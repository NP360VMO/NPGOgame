Mon Profile - Cable Cloud (Standard Mon)
Sky Traveler · Steady · Friendly

🗺️ Introduction:

Meet Cable Cloud (Standard Mon), the energetic, chattiest buddy zooming through Ngong Ping’s skies in a lively cable car! Full with energy and laughs, this lively pal scoops up travellers from all over, soaring over rolling hills and sparkly straits from Tung Chung to Ngong Ping.

Every 25-minute ride is more than just breathtaking views — it’s a joyful adventure through history and culture!

Standard Mon’s the ultimate talker, dishing out Ngong Ping’s charm and quirky tales with a goofy grin. From the grand Big Buddha to the chill Po Lin Monastery and the bustling Ngong Ping Village, her stories make you laugh like you’re besties with Lantau Island’s soul!

Whether you're watching planes zoom in at Hong Kong International Airport or gasping at the stunning mountain-and-sea views, Crystal offers a fun, happy adventure that captures Ngong Ping's cool spirit!

🗺️ Mon Scoop

The Ngong Ping 360 Standard Cable Car features a diamond-shaped design with panoramic windows, allowing passengers to enjoy a 360-degree view of Lantau Island’s natural scenery.
