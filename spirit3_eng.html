Panoramic Guardian · 360°

🗺️ Introduction: Crystal+

Say hi to Crystal +, the cutest, sparkly buddy zooming through Ngong Ping’s skies as a see-through panoramic cable car! This adorable pal loves hanging out way up high, giggling at the fluffy clouds, twinkly seas, and majestic mountains, being the sweetest guide for all you explorers!

Zipping 5.7 kilometers from Tung Chung to Ngong Ping, this 25-minute sky adventure feels like a magical cloud dance with Lantau Island’s heart! Crystal +’s totally clear cabin gives you a super-duper 360° view, showing off mountains, waters, and city lights in the most dazzling way!

As the chattiest storytelling Mon, Crystal + spills Lantau’s tales with a big grin—from the awesome Tian Tan Buddha to the cozy Po Lin Monastery and the bubbly Ngong Ping Village.

Crystal+ wraps you in a glittery, heart-happy sky party, making every moment a total cutie-fest!

🗺️ Mon Scoop: Crystal+ Cable Car

The Ngong Ping 360 Panoramic Cable Car, Crystal+, is a see-through superstar with a glass floor and walls, giving you over 80% crystal-clear views! This zippy buddy carries up to 10 pals for a sky-high giggle-fest!
