🗺️ Mon Scoop: Bubble Tea & Egg Waffle Fun

Bubble tea popped up in Taiwan in the 1980s, mixing frothy tea with chewy tapioca pearls for a super fun drink that’s now a worldwide fave!

Egg waffles were born in Hong Kong in the 1950s when clever vendors turned broken eggs and batter into crispy, bubbly treats. Now they’re the cutest stars of Hong Kong’s street food scene!
