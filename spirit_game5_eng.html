Spirit Introduction - Doggy Kim
Street · Happiness

🗺️ Introduction:

Doggy Kim is a regional spirit based on the Hong Kong Tong Dog, with golden fur and gentle eyes, a dog-like spirit!

He has a simple and cute personality, likes to be close to humans, and is especially used to sleeping near police posts or street corners. He is a warm guardian silently existing in the community.

🗺️ Spirit Knowledge

As long as someone calls out "Doggy Kim~", he will wake up immediately and run to the caller wagging his tail. Although he seems to sleep all day, he actually has a subtle perception of the surrounding environment and people's emotions.

It is said that gently rubbing behind his ears will bring a day of good luck and a sense of security.
