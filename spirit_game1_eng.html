🗺️ Mon Scoop: Southern Yellow Cattle

The Southern Yellow Cattle is a super cute member of China’s three big yellow cattle breeds, hanging out in southern spots like Guangdong, Fujian, Hainan, and even around Lantau Island!

These adorable feral cattle love to wander the fields in Lantau’s cozy rural areas, like Mui Wo and Lamma Island. Spot them chilling in the grass, adding a sweet, natural vibe to your travels!

✅ Proper Ways to Interact with Cattle

  • Stay calm and quiet, avoiding loud shouts or impulsive actions to prevent startling the cattle.
  • Keep a safe distance from the cattle, and do not touch, chase, or pat them, especially calves; mother cows may attack to protect their young.

🚫 Behaviors to Avoid Around Cattle

  • Do not feed them any food, including vegetables, meat, or processed foods.
  • Avoid using flash photography, especially in low-light conditions, to prevent startling them.
  • Do not make loud noises, create disturbances, or harass cattle with objects.
